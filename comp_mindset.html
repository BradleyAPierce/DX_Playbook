What's in it for....

At this point in our exploration, we've defined and explored the associated behaviors of an individual with a growth mindset. We even looked at an example of how a growth mindset would "show up" in our daily work. In the next section we'll explore the benefits of a Growth Mindset and share what's in it for you, your team, and Konica Minolta.

You?



Rollover to scroll information

You?

  • Ongoing personal and professional development increases your knowledge about your business and your customers.
  • Expanded knowledge and a broader perspective allows you to look at challenges in a broader and more effective way.
  • Continuous improvement in your daily work facilitates growth, productivity and brings value to how work gets done.
  • Increased confidence and a new sense of resilience. Fear of uncertainty and insurmountable challenges dissipates.

Your team?



Rollover to scroll information

Your team?

  • Accepting and incorporating feedback, knowledge sharing and support in the face of failure allows your team to grow and develop.
  • Your team will perceive change in a more positive way and will develop demonstrable ways to adapt to change.
  • Your team will be more flexible to unexpected changes and therefore more skilled at moving projects forward to completion.

Konica Minolta?



Rollover to scroll information

Konica Minolta?

  • Your team will confront challenges with Increased agility and ability to pivot. Challenges will be resolved more expeditiously, allowing company strategy to be executed more rapidly.
  • Your team's performance will improve as a result of improved flexibility and expanded capabilities. Performance improvement will increase the efficiency of business operations.
  • Adoption of a culture of continuous learning and performance improvement will inspire others to grow and improve business outcomes.

The Growth Mindset Journey

As you develop a Growth Mindset your thinking, behavior and skill set also grows incrementally. The journey will look something like this:

Starting Your Growth Mindset Journey


Click the play button to listen to the audio.

You will become curious and gain an interest in new areas of personal and professional growth.

Continuing Your Growth Mindset Journey


Click the play button to listen to the audio.

You will embrace challenges on the job as learning opportunities and persevere, rather than give up. Soon you will seek out opportunities to grow and eventually inspire others to do the same! You will adapt to change quickly and incorporate new ideas to solve problems. You will welcome feedback and utilize it as a means to improve performance at work.

Developing a Growth Mindset for High Impact


Click the play button to listen to the audio.

Soon you will share examples of success and failure in order to coach and mentor others in their development. Your humble and agile approach to learning will enable you to create a culture of learning and experimentation for others.

Think About This: Which statement reflects your stage in the Growth Mindset journey?

Statement 1: I actively explore new areas to learn, build skills and grow.

Statement 2: I look for and accept opportunities to learn in my everyday work and inspire my colleagues to do the same.

Statement 3: I share my success and failures with my colleagues with the goal of coaching and mentoring them as they grow professionally.
