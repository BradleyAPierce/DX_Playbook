What is our DX Vertical Market Strategy?

Marco Maggio and Chris Bilello discuss Konica Minolta's and DX Sales Vertical's approaches. They talk about the importance of having vertical expertise and the actual value it brings to our customers.

Legal Vertical Market

Zina Motley-Weaver and Ryan Conlan explain how DX can improve the legal industry by making the process more efficient, secure, and customer-focused. They mention several advantages, such as automation of tasks, increased security, improved customer experience, and better access to data and analytics. Additionally, they discuss how Konica Minolta and All Covered have been working together to develop solutions for the legal industry, including hardware and software portfolios suitable for the legal market, professional and IT services, and automation technology. The presentation concludes with the importance of streamlining operations and embracing digital solutions to remain competitive.
