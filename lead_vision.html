What's in it for....

Now that you have a better understanding of what it means to have Vision, let's take a look at the benefits. What's In it for you, your team and Konica Minolta?

You?

You?

  • You will understand the direction and strategy of the organization and align your work to participate in goal achievement.
  • You will clearly communicate company strategy to team members so that they will understand and engage in work that drives results and contributes to positive business outcomes.
  • You will broaden your purview of work to include forward thinking activities and projects that align with company values, mission and strategy.

Your team?

Your team?

  • Your team will understand and implement strategy in projects that achieve positive business outcomes.
  • Your team will benefit from collaborating on how strategy can be optimally implemented to achieve company goals.
  • Your team will model and replicate how business strategy can be shared, executed and measured to produce tangible results.
  • Your team will generate interest and optimism as vision is shared and successfully implemented.

Konica Minolta?

Konica Minolta?

  • You build coalitions and business alliances to share company vision so that impactful and measurable programs are developed, deployed and replicated.

The Vision Journey

Vision facilitates an understanding of how events, people and processes impact the organization on a global scale. It also allows broad-based problem solving and idea creation to take place so that true impact is realized. The journey looks something like this:

Starting Your Vision Journey


Click the play button to listen to the audio.

The ability to transform vision from ideas into tangible and impactful business results starts with an understanding of company strategy and direction. It also starts with a commitment to embrace and communicate that strategy in practical and forward thinking terms. As you develop proficiency in communicating company vision, your perspective broadens from describing individual strategic initiatives to articulating corporate initiatives. Gradually, you discover ways to implement that strategy and share those ideas with your team.

Continuing Your Vision Journey


Click the play button to listen to the audio.

As your journey progresses, your team members become optimistic and inspired to implement programs that advance company vision. You introduce innovative programs that align with our Digital Transformation and are practical and measurable. You extend your work to leaders across the organization to accelerate goal achievement. Alliances with key stakeholders expand both internally and externally. You continuously monitor and reinforce alignment with company mission and goals.

Developing Vision for High Impact


Click the play button to listen to the audio.

As you develop and implement company strategy, you immediately incorporate an understanding of how the organization connects interdepartmentally and globally. You create measurable impact in bringing strategy to life in daily work and empower others to do the same. Your work is in line with the future direction of the company and reflects a deep understanding of market trends and customer needs. Your business strategy is shared and successfully replicated throughout the larger organization.

Think About This: Which statement reflects your stage in the Vision journey?

Statement 1: I understand our company's long-term strategy and can communicate it effectively.

Statement 2: I communicate our company's strategy to team members and incorporate those initiatives into our work and culture.

Statement 3: I have successfully executed company strategy and have empowered others to do the same.
