What's in it for....

Now that you have a better understanding of what it means to have Adaptability, let's take a look at the benefits. What's In it for you, your team and Konica Minolta?

You?



Rollover to scroll information

You?

  • You will learn how to utilize new methods and behaviors to address change in a more positive and productive way.
  • You will build resilience as you learn to flexibly tackle uncertainty and volatility.
  • You will comfortably question and revisit ideas and approaches, and adjust strategy when circumstances warrant change.
  • You will evaluate and determine the best course of action based on the demands of the situation in order to attain positive outcomes.

Your team?



Rollover to scroll information

Your team?

  • You will view and communicate change with optimism and a sense of urgency, while providing support to team members who are adopting and integrating behavior change.
  • You will recognize resistance to change and role model behavior that demonstrates the value of adopting change when encountering barriers and obstacles to goal attainment.
  • Your team will effectively analyze the impact of change and pivot accordingly to improve the customer experience and advance business initiatives.
  • Your team will adjust actions quickly, when needed, to deliver solutions for advancing work while overcoming obstacles to progress.

Konica Minolta?



Rollover to scroll information

Konica Minolta?

  • You will achieve positive business outcomes as a result of adapting quickly and flexibly to changes in customer requirements, shifts in the business, and market trends.
  • You will role model behavior that supports a culture that successfully adopts and implements change in order to achieve positive business results.
  • The benefits of adopting and implementing changes to processes, problem resolution and environmental change will be realized throughout the organization.
  • The organization will be better equipped to meet future business challenges as a result of flexibly addressing unanticipated change.

The Adaptability Journey

Adaptability enables swift and strategic adjustments to thinking and behavior in order to respond effectively to change so that business goals and initiatives may be achieved. The journey looks something like this:

Starting Your Adaptability Journey


Click the play button to listen to the audio.

You will adopt and demonstrate a positive attitude towards change of all kinds and embrace the concept that change is constant and has a positive impact on the business environment, processes, strategy and growth.You reconsider your ideas and behavior to support changing conditions and advance business initiatives.

Continuing Your Adaptability Journey


Click the play button to listen to the audio.

Gradually, you instinctively adjust to new situations and circumstances by adopting new methods, procedures and tools to navigate change. You pivot quickly and flexibly to deliver solutions for advancing work while overcoming obstacles. You demonstrably role model the positive results of implementing change and building resilience to team members, and assist them with integrating behaviors to successfully pilot change in daily work.

Developing Adaptability for High Impact


Click the play button to listen to the audio.

You enthusiastically promote and demonstrate the value of embracing change as a part of daily work across the organization. You anticipate and assimilate change by continuously scanning the internal and external business environment. You modify behavior and strategy to incorporate change and pivot quickly to address obstacles to positive business outcomes. Your ability to communicate and confront change with positivism and a sense of urgency produces tangible business results that are recognized and replicated across the organization.
