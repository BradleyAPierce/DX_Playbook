Leadership Drivers Deep Dives

It is essential that leaders across the organization develop and demonstrate Konica Minolta's Core Competencies. Beyond that foundation, a second set of competencies will drive a successful Digital Transformation. Those competencies are Leadership Drivers. Leadership Drivers layer Core Competencies with behaviors that foster a vision for Konica Minolta that colleagues and team members can embrace and deploy. Let's take a deeper dive into each of the Leadership Drivers to learn more.

1. Growth Mindset

Growth Mindset is a foundational competency that underpins the success of the Konica Minolta DX Journey. We begin with Growth Mindset because this competency will lead you to embrace challenges on the job and welcome feedback as a means to grow and improve your capabilities.

Go to Deep Dive

2. Customer Centricity

Customer Centricity is a second foundational competency that keeps our focus on our customers in everything we do and every decision we make. Our corporate philosophy states:

"We exist solely for our customers; always thinking on their behalf…"

Go to Deep Dive

3. Engaging People

Engaging People is an essential pillar for anyone who aspires to lead others. Our future rests on how we encourage our colleagues to contribute to Konica Minolta's strategic initiatives.




Go to Deep Dive

4. Developing People

Developing People means that you commit yourself to the success of your colleagues. You do this through actions such as providing constructive feedback, helping team members explore growth opportunities, and setting challenging goals.
