Driving Digital Transformation Course

Driving Digital Transformation - Konica Minolta's Core Competencies and Leadership Drivers describes the impact of digital transformation (DX) on Konica Minolta's business and culture. Learners will gain a high level understanding of how DX is driving a strategy that calls for all employees to embrace skills and behaviors that support change and sustained growth. Konica Minolta's Global set of Core Competencies and Leadership Drivers provide the foundation for developing the mindset and behaviors of future-ready leaders.
