DX Solutions Strategy

Click the play button to listen to the audio.

Konica Minolta's DX Solutions Strategy is fueled by our commitment to execute Dual Transformation - maximize the core, while creating the new. In other words, as we maximize our core business, we will simultaneously create and present new, innovative and profitable solutions to our customers who are also navigating Digital Transformation. These solutions align with areas of the business that are experiencing significant growth, and include:

• Intelligent Information Management (IIM)
• Video Solutions Services (VSS)
• Managed IT Services (MIT)
• The Digital Workplace

As we assist our customers with their own transformation, we must first assess their current state of digital maturity, and then introduce them to solutions from our rich portfolio of products and services that will enable them to improve communication, processes, revenue generation, and ultimately, business results.

Digital Maturity

Describes an organization's ability to implement digital technologies and processes to achieve its strategic objectives and stay competitive. Assessing Digital Maturity includes the evaluation of digital capabilities across various areas of an organization, including its technology, strategy, operations, customer experience and culture.
