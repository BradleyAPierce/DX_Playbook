What's in it for....

So far you have explored behaviors of individuals who embrace Business Thinking. You have viewed an example shared by a Konica Minolta colleague. Next, dive into some benefits of Business Thinking for you, your team, and Konica Minolta.

You?

You?

  • Your understanding of business strategy will help you align your role to the organization’s goals.
  • You could receive recognition for the work you perform that produces better business outcomes.
  • You contribute ideas and action plans that impact the business in a positive way.

Your team?

Your team?

  • Your ideas to influence team efficiency and productivity will make your team a valuable and high-impact business asset.
  • You establish a team mindset that is focused on achieving successful business results across the organization.
  • You role model behavior for others seeking to contribute to the success of the business.
  • Your ability to incorporate market trends, data and technology improves your team's ability to implement impactful solutions for our customers.

The Business Thinking Journey

As you develop your skill in Business Thinking, your thinking and behavior transform. Your journey will look something like this:

Starting Your Business Thinking Journey


Click the play button to listen to the audio.

You will begin by developing a deep understanding of our current strategy, our portfolio of products and services, our customer journey, and our business processes. This understanding will help you align your work to our business goals. You will also become familiar with the challenges - both internal and external - that our business faces, and steps we can take to overcome them.

Continuing Your Business Thinking Journey


Click the play button to listen to the audio.

Working collaboratively with your team, you will propose and implement a strategy to develop and deliver solutions that contribute to the success of the organization. You will engage your team to evaluate the pros and cons of new initiatives, so that you can proactively align resources to ensure a successful business outcome. You will continue to challenge your team to incorporate data insights, technological advancements and process improvements to develop innovative solutions to solve business challenges.

Developing Business Thinking for High Impact


Click the play button to listen to the audio.

You will use business thinking to integrate our strategy and financial goals into your projects and create a positive environment for achieving results. Your contributions to deploying solutions, improving business processes and developing new business opportunities will have positive and demonstrable impact across the organization.

Think About This: Which statement reflects your stage in the Business Thinking journey?

Statement 1: I am at the start of my journey and currently focus on gaining understanding of our business strategy and our customer journey.

Statement 2: I am continuing my journey and I now use business thinking in my collaborative work on team projects.

Statement 3: I have developed and implemented new ways to overcome business challenges and make a positive impact on the organization.

Statement 4: I have motivated individuals across the organization to identify and implement business improvements and solutions that provide value to the organization.
