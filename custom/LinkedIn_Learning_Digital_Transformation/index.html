Digital Transformation

Digital Transformation is well underway with technology like the Internet of Things (IoT), the cloud, and Artificial Intelligence (AI) making a strong impact on how companies do business, and how they interact with their customers. To stay relevant and compete effectively, organizations are reevaluating their processes, their technology and their culture, as they adopt new tools for working and communicating more effectively. In this course, you will learn what Digital Transformation (DX) is and how it evolves from concept to implementation within an organization. You will learn about the disruptive technologies that are moving DX initiatives forward, as well as the blockers that keep companies from making progress.
