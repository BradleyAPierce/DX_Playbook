The Building Alliances Journey

As you develop your ability in Building Alliances, your skills also grow incrementally. The journey will look something like this:

Starting Your Building Alliances Journey

You will recognize opportunities to start new partnerships with others outside your team or department. You will build trusting relationships with colleagues from your existing network by sharing ideas and leveraging their knowledge to improve your own work.

Continuing Your Building Alliances Journey

You will experience a new level of success when you collaborate with others in a cross functional team to build a more robust deliverable. Soon you will easily recognize opportunities to partner with others in multi-stakeholder alliances to achieve new outcomes that impact a significant segment of the business. You will continue to align with other teams and working groups to create solutions that improve business operations.

Developing Building Alliances for High Impact

You will influence your co-workers by providing direction on the types of partnership opportunities to pursue. You will support your team by facilitating successful alliances and assisting in solving challenges that obstruct successful alliances. You will lead by recommending rules of engagement for establishing productive long-term alliances. You will be a role model because you can demonstrate best practices in forging partnerships both within and outside Konica Minolta.

Think About This: Which statement reflects your stage in the Building Alliances journey?

Statement 1: I am looking forward to an opportunity to build an alliance with colleagues from another functional area of the organization.

Statement 2: I have worked with team members to start and sustain a new partnership with Konica Minolta coworkers outside my functional area. We are working on a plan to solve a business problem that is a shared concern.

Statement 3: I have guided and coached my team to create and sustain alliances with other functional teams within Konica Minolta to tackle and solve shared challenges.

Statement 4: I have positive experiences building alliances with project stakeholders both within and outside Konica Minolta. These alliances have already resulted in mutually beneficial business opportunities.
