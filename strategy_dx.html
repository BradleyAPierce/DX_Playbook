Our DX Strategy: Driving DX Through Dual Transformation

Konica Minolta's DX Strategy is a manifestation of our company's vision statement "to transform the work experience and connect people, technology and information that drives unprecedented customer success". Our vision statement is a commitment to both our employees as well as our customers to utilize technology to innovate and provide solutions that will enable us, and our customers to digitally transform, remain relevant, competitive and successful both now and well into the future. Our strategy is our plan to execute and actualize our vision. How is that accomplished? By implementing Dual Transformation. Dual Transformation advances DX by maximizing our core business while simultaneously creating innovative and profitable solutions for our customers.
